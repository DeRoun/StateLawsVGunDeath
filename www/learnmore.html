The Multiple Cause of Death data available on CDC WONDER are county-level national mortality and population data. Data are based on death certificates for U.S. residents. Each death certificate contains a single underlying cause of death, up to twenty additional multiple causes, and demographic data.

This Data Viz application would not be possible without the amazing datasets provided by both the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) and the great folks who put together the State Firearm Law Database. See more information about them below
